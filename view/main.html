However, "outsourcing" is not a popular vocabulary for internal and external businesses. It is a word that many professional development companies avoid. Instead, "high-end customization", "customized development", "professional bespoke development companies" are more likely to be used. For the customers, I found impressions on the Internet: sweet-talking, poor quality, copying code...

It's no wonder that most technology services companies don't want to define themselves as outsourcing companies, and such a popular impression is just a part of the phenomenon, but it's an objective fact.

The common problems in the software outsourcing industry are:

Resource Mismatching
Outsourcing companies have a lot of reliable teams. I know a lot of technical teams come out to start a business, but most of them have struggled for a few years, still on the edge of a small scale, skilled, but not able to keep up with their business ability.

Price Confusion
Outsourcing platforms such as Freelancer reduce market development price by one level. In a market with price confusion and oversupply, customers who do not know much about software development always pay too much attention to the price, but neglect the quality of project delivery which cannot be predicted in advance.

Different design, technical implementation, software delivery quality and after-sale service standard could lead to the cost gap of project development being very large for the same function list of quotations.

These schemas usually don't have a clear standard before selling. In an industry with high cost of personnel, if a project received at a low price is often ended with a bad end, it is the inevitable result of a vicious price competition.

Subcontracting
Because the business throughput and carrying capacity of each company is limited and stable, and the instability in business has caused many projects to be digested in the form of subcontracting. Especially for companies with strong business ability, subcontracting is a very common phenomenon.

In order to guarantee the profit of the project, subcontracting usually can only find small teams, small companies, and even part-time individual contractors, so it can also easily lead to many problems, such as control of development management, quality control, communication and so on.

These schemas usually don't have a clear standard before selling. In an industry with high cost of personnel, if a project received at a low price is often ended with a bad end, it is the inevitable result of a vicious price competition.

Low Industry Profit
Many outsiders feel that software outsourcing is very profitable, but the situation in the line is that the profits of the software outsourcing companies are very low, and the survival and profit of the industry are not optimistic.

For a project, 10% of the cost for sales, 10% for marketing promotion costs, 20% for personnel management, the remaining 50-60% is artificial and site equipment costs. If management or business is slightly unable to keep up, the profit part is basically lost.

In order to survive, most companies can only increase their marketing investment, reduce the standard of software services. An industry that does not have a way to make a profit, usually does not have much patience to care for the quality of the project.

Then, what is an ideal software outsourcing service? I put forward the following four standards:

Communication before sales, no sweet-talking
Some salesmen give people the impression of "sweet-talking" and "distrust". I think there are two reasons: one is excessive marketing and "harassment", exaggerated propaganda, too much attention to their own interests, and sometimes it is counterproductive; the two is not enough professionalism, especially for technical feasibility and project design, often give the plan and evaluation lacks professional and feasible support.

A successful sale must be honest and trustworthy, first solve the problem of business trust, and then stand in the perspective of professional and customer, output a reasonable solution, take the reasonable price to help the customer to solve the actual problems, but not to make more money to put forward the self advantageous plan, or even the wrong guidance.

In order to survive, most companies can only increase their marketing investment, reduce the standard of software services. An industry that does not have a way to make a profit, usually does not have much patience to care for the quality of the project.

Design professional, understand innovation
A good design is half the project's success. This is also a very difficult part, especially in the stage of the project prototype design. The product manager needs to understand the business, design the interaction and interface prototype of the whole project in combination with the actual scene and operation of the users. Knowing to appropriately innovating, combining the actual project scene with the special design, the customization can reflect its meaning and value. There are a of the projects spend the customization money, but are carrying out in copying code way.

Excellent skill, leaving no hidden defects
I have met many unfinished projects have to be overthrown again, and several secondhand projects have been rebuilt. Technology skill is the intrinsic basic work of the project. A good technical framework and rigorous and elegant code logic can save a lot of cost for the subsequent iterative development and maintenance.

Why do many outsourcing companies not willing to take over other companies' secondhand projects? Why were some of the outsourcing companies careless with projects after release?

Because the project has too many portential issues which leads to operation and maintenance cost is too high. Technical person did not make a good architecture design, plus the uneven quality of the developers, the changes in requirements, which result in the project quality is tasteless but wasteful to discard.

For the choice of the software outsourcing partner, the technical strength must be the most important standard. It is related to the internal quality of the project, the stability of the function, the cost of operation and maintenance. And more importantly, it decides that the project needs to be overthrown or not.

After-sales service in place
After sales service is often the end of the outsourcing company's service, but it is the starting point for the operation of the project owner. Outsourcing company begins to turn from passive disadvantage to initiative advantage, which is a link to test outsourcing company's professional ethics.

For an outsouring company with professional conduct, even if project owner has no restrictions on him, when encountered problems, in the scope of the contract, the company will also strictly carry out the obligations and responsibilities that they need to perform.

The above mentioned the problems in the software outsourcing industry, but this is not the essence of outsourcing industry.

I think the good development of an industry depends on the outsider has a comprehensive and objective understanding of it. It also needs to strengthen self-discipline, optimize the service process, improve the quality of service, and also, a more important point is to find some innovative and sustainable development direction. This is the direction I have been trying and working hard for the past few years.

Fortunately, there are still many such software service companies, but most of them exist in a small and beautiful way, but not well known by the public.
